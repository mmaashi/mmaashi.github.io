## Real Estate / Renting (العقارات)

### Key Platforms

| Platform | Type |
|----------|------|
| **Haraj** (حراج) | Largest classifieds (haraj.com.sa) |
| **Property Finder** | UAE-focused but expanding KSA |
| **Bayut** | Property listings |
| **Sakani** | Government housing program |

### Rental Process

1. **Search** — Online platforms or real estate agent
2. **View** — Visit property
3. **Negotiate** — Price often negotiable (5-15%)
4. **Contract** — 1-year contract, usually paid annually or semi-annually
5. **Ejari** — Registration with municipality (required)
6. **Utilities** — Connect after Ejari

### Key Terms

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **Cheque** | Post-dated checks (standard payment) |
| **Ejari** | Rental contract registration |
| **Molhakat** | Municipality lease registration |
| **Wafi** | Commission to agent (1 month rent typical) |

## Healthcare (الرعاية الصحية)

### System Overview

Saudi Arabia has a rapidly growing healthcare sector with both public and private options.

### Public Healthcare

- **Free for Saudi nationals**
- **Expats:** Can access public hospitals but private insurance is strongly recommended
- **Costs:** Affordable but long wait times

## Visa & Residency (التأشيرات والإقامة)

### Types of Residency

| Type | Duration | Requirements |
|------|----------|--------------|
| **Employment Visa** | 1-2 years | Job offer, sponsored by employer |
| **Investor Visa** | 2-5 years | Investment in Saudi company/property |
| **Premium Residency** | 5-10 years | $800K+ investment or $35K+ salary |
| **Family Visit** | 3-12 months | Sponsored by resident |

### Iqama (Residence Permit)

- **Cost:** ~SAR 500-1,000 (varies)
- **Duration:** 1 year (renewable)
- **Process:** Sponsored by employer or investor

## Lifestyle & Culture

### What to Expect

- **Conservative Dress:** Modest clothing expected (especially women)
- **Prayer Times:** Businesses close 5x daily (15-30 min)
- **Weekend:** Friday-Saturday (Friday is holy day)
- **Alcohol:** Banned (except for non-Muslims in certain areas)
- **Gender Segregation:** Rare in international settings, common in local
- **Ramadan:** Business slows significantly

### Pros

- ✅ High salary potential (tax-free)
- ✅ Safe country (very low crime)
- ✅ Modern infrastructure
- ✅ Growing entertainment options
- ✅ Strategic location

### Challenges

- ⚠️ Hot climate (45°C+ summer)
- ⚠️ Limited social freedoms
- ⚠️ Cultural adjustment
- ⚠️ Language barrier (Arabic preferred)
- ⚠️ Dependent on employer for visa

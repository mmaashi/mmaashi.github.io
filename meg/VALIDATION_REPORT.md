## كيف سنوفر الخدمة؟ | How We Deliver

### Flow Chart | خريطة العمليات

```
Client visits MEG website
        ↓
Completes eligibility quiz (5 min)
        ↓
Gets personalized checklist (FREE)
        ↓
[Option A] Client does it themselves → Free value, we get data
        ↓
[Option B] Client wants help → Pay for Premium
        ↓

        ↓
PRO does the work
        ↓
Client gets result
        ↓
We take margin (200%)
```

### من سيقوم بالعمل الفعلي؟ | Who Does the Work?

| Role | Who | Cost to Us |
|------|-----|------------|

### لماذا سيعمل هذا؟ | Why This Works

1. **No physical office needed** — Partners have office
2. **No local employees** — Partners have staff
3. **Scalable** — Add more partners, not staff
4. **Low risk** — Partners bear operational risk
5. **High margin** — We control client relationship

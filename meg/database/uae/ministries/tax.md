# UAE Federal Tax Authority (الهيئة الاتحادية للضرائب)

> **Last Updated:** February 2026
> **Data Sources:** Official FTA website (tax.gov.ae), UAE government sources

---

## Overview

The Federal Tax Authority (FTA) was established in 2017 to administer federal taxes in the UAE. It oversees:

- **VAT** (Value Added Tax) — 5%
- **Corporate Tax** — 9% on business income
- **Excise Tax** — on tobacco, energy drinks, carbonated drinks
- **Digital Services Tax**

---

## Corporate Tax (ضريبة الشركات)

### Overview

UAE Corporate Tax was introduced in June 2023 with a 9% rate on business income exceeding AED 375,000.

### Key Details

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Rate** | 9% on taxable income above AED 375,000 |
| **Threshold** | AED 375,000 per year (taxable income) |
| **Below Threshold** | 0% rate |
| **Free Zone Income** | 0% (qualifying free zone income) |
| **Financial Year** | Gregorian or Hijri (must be consistent) |

### Free Zone Exemption

Free Zone entities can enjoy 0% corporate tax on qualifying income if they:
1. Maintain adequate substance in the UAE Free Zone
2. Derive qualifying income (not mainland UAE income)
3. Meet transfer pricing requirements
4. Do not have mainland UAE "relevant activities" income above thresholds

### Exemptions

- Natural resource extraction (governed by emirate-level authorities)
- Dividends and capital gains from qualifying shareholdings
- Foreign currency exchange gains
- Exempted investment funds

### Filing Requirements

| Type | Deadline |
|------|----------|
| Tax Return | 9 months after end of financial year |
| Self-Assessment | Yes, with advance payment |
| Documentation | Keep records for at least 7 years |

## VAT (Value Added Tax) (ضريبة القيمة المضافة)

### Rates

| Rate | Application |
|------|-------------|
| 0% | Essential foods, healthcare, education, public transport |
| 5% | Standard rate on most goods and services |
| 100% | Excise tax on tobacco, energy drinks, sugary drinks |

### Registration Threshold

| Type | Threshold |
|------|-----------|
| Mandatory | AED 375,000 annual taxable supplies |
| Voluntary | AED 150,000 annual taxable supplies |
| Free Zone | Same thresholds apply |

### VAT Compliance

- **Tax Return:** Quarterly (or monthly for large taxpayers)
- **Input VAT:** Recoverable on business purchases
- **Output VAT:** Collected on supplies
- **Reverse Charge:** Applies on certain goods/services

## Excise Tax (ضريبة الانتقائية)

### Products

| Product | Tax Rate |
|---------|----------|
| Tobacco products | 100% |
| Energy drinks | 100% |
| Carbonated drinks | 50% |
| Sweetened beverages | 50% |

### Registration

Mandatory for producers, importers, and stockpilers of excise goods.

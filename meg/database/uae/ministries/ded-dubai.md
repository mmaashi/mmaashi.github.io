# Dubai Department of Economic Development (DED) (اقتصادية دبي)

> **Last Updated:** February 2026
> **Data Sources:** Official DED Dubai website (ded.gov.ae), Dubai government sources

---

## Overview

The Department of Economic Development (DED) is the principal authority for business setup and commercial activities in Dubai (excluding free zones). It regulates:
- Company registration
- Commercial licensing
- Business activity permits
- Economic policy

## License Types in Dubai

### 1. Commercial License (رخصة تجارية)

For trading activities (import, export, distribution, retail, wholesale)

**Sub-categories:**
- General Trading
- Specific Trading (restricted items)
- Commodity Trading
- Export/Import

**Cost:** AED 2,000 - 50,000+ depending on activity

### 2. Professional License (رخصة مهنية)

For professional services (consulting, services, crafts)

**Examples:**
- Management Consultancy
- Technical Consultancy
- Media Services
- Legal Services

**Cost:** AED 1,000 - 15,000

### 3. Industrial License (رخصة صناعية)

For manufacturing and industrial activities

**Requirements:**
- Factory location approval
- Environmental clearance
- Industrial registration

**Cost:** AED 5,000 - 100,000+

### 4. Tourism License (رخصة سياحية)

For tourism-related activities

**Cost:** AED 5,000 - 50,000+

## Setup Process

### Step-by-Step

1. **Determine Activity** — Choose business activity code
2. **Choose Name** — Trade name reservation (ded.gov.ae)
3. **Initial Approval** — Submit online, receive in 2-48 hours
4. **Lease Agreement** — Office/warehouse space contract
5. **Submit Documents** — Full application with all documents
6. **Pay Fees** — License fee, flexi-desk or office rental
7. **Receive License** — 2-5 business days (instant for some)
8. **Open Bank Account** — 2-4 weeks
9. **Apply for Visa** — 2-4 weeks

### Documents Required

- Passport copies of shareholders
- National ID (for UAE residents)
- Business plan (for certain activities)
- Lease agreement (Ejari)
- No objection certificate (for existing UAE residents)
- Educational certificates (for professional licenses)
- Board resolution (for companies)

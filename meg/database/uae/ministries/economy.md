## Role & Jurisdiction

The Ministry of Economy is the primary federal authority for:
- Economic planning and development
- Company law and commercial regulations
- Foreign investment promotion
- Competition policy
- Intellectual property rights
- Statistics and economic research

---

## Foreign Investment Regulations

### Investment Law

The UAE enacted Federal Decree-Law No. 19 of 2018 (Foreign Investment Law) which allows 100% foreign ownership in most sectors (except specific negative list activities).

### Company Registration for Foreign Entities

Foreign companies can establish:

| Entity Type | Arabic | Key Features |
|-------------|--------|--------------|
| **Limited Liability Company (LLC)** | شركة ذات مسؤولية محدودة | Most common. Requires 1-50 partners. Minimum capital varies by activity |
| **Public Joint Stock Company (PJSC)** | شركة مساهمة عامة | Minimum capital AED 2M, minimum 5 shareholders |
| **Private Joint Stock Company (PrJSC)** | شركة مساهمة خاصة | Minimum capital AED 500K, 2-200 shareholders |
| **Branch Office** | فرع شركة أجنبية | Extension of foreign parent company |
| **Representative Office** | مكتب تمثيلي | Marketing/ liaison only, cannot generate revenue |

### Commercial Registration Requirements

1. Trade name reservation (via DED)
2. Initial approval from Ministry of Economy
3. Articles of Association (for LLC/JSC)
4. Lease agreement for office space
5. Passport copies of shareholders
6. No objection certificate (if existing UAE resident)
7. Manager's appointment letter

---

## Key Requirements by Activity Type

### Trading/Commercial Activities
- Trade license from Department of Economic Development (DED)
- Approval from relevant authority (e.g., Ministry of Economy for general trading)
- Inventory list for certain activities

### Professional/Services
- Professional license from DED
- Educational qualifications attestation
- No office space required for some freelance permits

### Industrial/Manufacturing
- Industrial license from Ministry of Economy
- Factory location approval (UAE Industrial Directory registration)
- Environmental clearance (if applicable)

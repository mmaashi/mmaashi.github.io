## Why Invest in UAE?

### Key Opportunities

- **Free Zones** — 40+ free zones with tax benefits
- **Golden Visa** — 10-year residence for investors
- **Strategic Location** — Gateway to Middle East/Asia
- **World-Class Infrastructure** — Transport, business
- **Easy Setup** — 5-15 days for company formation

### Key Challenges

- **Cost** — Higher cost of living than KSA
- **Competition** — Saturated markets
- **Visa Tied to Employment** — Need job/sponsor
- **Cultural Adjustment** — Different lifestyle

## Business Setup Overview

### Mainland vs. Free Zone

| Factor | Mainland | Free Zone |
|--------|----------|-----------|
| Ownership | 100% | 100% |
| Corporate Tax | 9% | 0% (qualifying) |
| Customs | 5% on imports | 0% |
| Location | Anywhere in UAE | One free zone only |
| Office | Required | Virtual available |

### Entity Types (Mainland)

| Type | Arabic | Best For |
|------|--------|----------|
| LLC | شركة ذات مسؤولية محدودة | Most businesses |
| JSC | شركة مساهمة | Public/private companies |
| Branch | فرع شركة | Extension of foreign co. |
| Representative | مكتب تمثيلي | Marketing only |

### Free Zone Types

| Zone Focus | Examples |
|------------|----------|
| Finance | DIFC, ADGM |
| Tech | Dubai Internet City |
| Media | Dubai Media City |
| Logistics | DAFZA, JAFZA |
| General | DMCC, IFZA |

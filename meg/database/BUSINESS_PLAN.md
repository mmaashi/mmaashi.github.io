# القسم الأول: الملخص التنفيذي | Executive Summary

## Vision | الرؤية

Become the definitive platform for foreign investors seeking to enter the Saudi Arabian market — the "LegalZoom for Saudi FDI."

## Mission | المهمة

Democratize access to Saudi regulatory intelligence by aggregating, translating, and presenting all ministry requirements in one AI-powered platform, reducing months of confusion to minutes of clarity.

## Problem | المشكلة

Foreign investors face a fragmented, multi-ministry landscape when entering Saudi Arabia:
- 6+ different ministries with separate requirements
- No centralized source of truth
- Language barriers (Arabic-only documentation)
- Lengthy consultant dependency ($5K-20K per entry)
- Unknown timelines and hidden costs

## Solution | الحل

An AI-powered platform that:
1. **Centralizes** all ministry regulations in one database
2. **Translates** requirements to English/Chinese (Phase 1)
3. **Automates** checklist generation for each investor type
4. **Connects** to local service providers (lawyers, accountants, PRO services)
5. **Tracks** application progress in real-time

# القسم الثالث: المنتج | Product

## Core Features | الميزات الأساسية

### 1. Ministry Intelligence Database
- Real-time aggregation of 6 ministry requirements
- Searchable, filtered by sector and entity type
- Version-controlled (tracks regulatory changes)

### 2. AI Eligibility Checker
- 5-minute questionnaire → personalized checklist
- "Can I open a tech company as 100% foreign owner?"
- Instant answer with citation to official regulations

### 3. Document Generator
- Auto-generates required forms based on eligibility check
- Arabic translations of foreign investor documents
- Templates for Articles of Association, board resolutions

### 4. Service Marketplace
- Verified local partners (law firms, PROs, accountants)
- Transparent pricing
- Integrated communication

### 5. Progress Tracker
- Real-time status of each application
- Timeline estimation
- Automated alerts for deadlines

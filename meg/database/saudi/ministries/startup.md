## Foreign Investment in the Startup Ecosystem

### Saudi Startup Visa / Entrepreneur Visa

Saudi Arabia has been developing programs to attract foreign entrepreneurs:

1. **Visiting Investor Visa** — available through Invest Saudi (investsaudi.sa)
   - For prospective investors exploring Saudi opportunities
   - Streamlined application via MISA platform
   
2. **Premium Residency** — المقيم المتميز
   - For high-value entrepreneurs and investors
   - Long-term residency without Saudi sponsor requirement
   - Managed by the Premium Residency Center

3. **Work Visa Sponsorship**
   - After company establishment, startups can sponsor foreign employees
   - Subject to Saudization (Nitaqat) quotas

### Starting a Company as a Foreign Entrepreneur

**Step-by-step process:**

1. **MISA Investment License** — apply through misa.gov.sa
2. **Commercial Registration** — register with Ministry of Commerce
3. **Choose entity type:**
   - **Simplified Joint Stock Company (SJSc)** — new form designed for startups (Companies Law 2023)
   - **LLC** — most common, no minimum capital
   - **Branch of foreign company** — for established foreign startups
4. **Bank Account** — open Saudi corporate bank account
5. **National Address** — register physical address
6. **Saudization** — plan Saudi workforce integration

## Business Incubator & Accelerator Requirements

### Licensing
- Business incubator licenses are issued by **Monsha'at** (monshaat.gov.sa/en/ep)
- Accelerator programs may also need CMA authorization if providing investment

### Notable Saudi Incubators/Accelerators
- **Flat6Labs Riyadh/Jeddah** — regional accelerator with Saudi operations
- **KAUST Innovation** — King Abdullah University of Science and Technology
- **Wa'ed** (Aramco's entrepreneurship arm)
- **BBAN** — Badir Program for Technology Incubators
- **Misk Innovation** — part of MiSK Foundation
- **Impact46** — Riyadh-based VC and accelerator
- **Raed Ventures** — early-stage VC

### Requirements for Foreign Incubator/Accelerator Operations

1. MISA Investment License
2. Monsha'at business incubator license (if operating as licensed incubator)
3. Commercial Registration
4. CMA authorization (if managing investment funds)
5. Physical presence in Saudi Arabia
6. Compliance with Saudization requirements

## Regulatory Environment

### Companies Law — Startup-Friendly Changes (2023+)

| Change | Impact |
|--------|--------|
| **No minimum capital for LLC** | Removes barrier to entry |
| **Simplified Joint Stock Company** | New entity type designed for startups with VC-friendly structure |
| **Single-person LLC** | Allowed — solo founders can incorporate |
| **Digital incorporation** | Fully online process through Ministry of Commerce |
| **Reduced compliance burden** | Simplified reporting for small companies |

### Intellectual Property Protection

- **SAIP** — Saudi Authority for Intellectual Property (https://www.saip.gov.sa)
- Patent, trademark, and copyright protection available
- Member of WIPO, Paris Convention, and Berne Convention
- Patent examination improving in speed and quality

### Tax Environment for Startups

| Tax | Rate | Notes |
|-----|------|-------|
| **Income Tax (foreign entities)** | 20% | On Saudi-source income |
| **Zakat (Saudi/GCC entities)** | 2.5% | On net worth basis |
| **VAT** | 15% | Standard rate since 2020 |
| **Withholding Tax** | 5-20% | On payments to non-residents |
| **Special Economic Zones** | Reduced rates | Available in designated zones |

## Important Notes for Foreign Entrepreneurs

1. **MISA license required** even for small startups — there is no exemption for startups
2. **Simplified JSC** is the recommended entity type for VC-backed startups
3. **Saudization applies** from day one — plan workforce mix accordingly
4. **VC-friendly structures** now available under updated Companies Law
5. **Government is a massive customer** — align product/service with Vision 2030 priorities
6. **Arabic language** requirements for legal documents remain
7. **Tax planning** — understand the difference between Zakat (for Saudi/GCC owners) and income tax (for foreign owners)
8. **Premium Residency** can simplify long-term presence for founders

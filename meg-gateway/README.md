# MEG — Market Entry Gateway (بوابة دخول السوق)

> Saudi Ministry Regulations Database for Foreign Investors

---

## Project Overview

MEG (Market Entry Gateway) is a comprehensive database of Saudi Arabian ministry regulations relevant to foreign investors entering the Saudi market. The goal is to centralize and structure all regulatory requirements, licensing procedures, and investment rules across key government ministries.

## Key Cross-Cutting Requirements

Every foreign investor in Saudi Arabia must navigate these common requirements regardless of sector:

1. **MISA Investment License** — mandatory first step for all foreign investors
2. **Commercial Registration** — via Ministry of Commerce
3. **Saudization (Nitaqat)** — Saudi workforce quotas
4. **Tax Registration** — ZATCA (Zakat, Tax and Customs Authority)
5. **National Address** — physical address registration
6. **Bank Account** — Saudi corporate bank account

## Data Methodology

- **Primary sources:** Official Saudi government websites (.gov.sa)
- **Secondary verification:** Wikipedia (referencing official decrees), official news agencies (SPA, Saudi Gazette)
- **Scope:** Current regulations as of February 2026
- **Limitations:** Many Saudi government websites are heavily JavaScript-rendered; some specific regulatory text was not directly extractable. Official websites and portals are cited for direct verification.

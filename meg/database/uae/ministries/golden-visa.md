# UAE Golden Visa (تأشيرة ذهبية)

> **Last Updated:** February 2026
> **Data Sources:** Official UAE government sources, Golden Visa program documentation

---

## Overview

The Golden Visa is a long-term (10-year) residence visa introduced in 2019 for investors, entrepreneurs, specialized talents, researchers, and outstanding students. It provides visa benefits for the holder and their family without needing a sponsor.

---

## Eligibility Categories

### 1. Investors (المستثمرون)

| Criteria | Requirement |
|----------|-------------|
| Property Investment | AED 2M+ in UAE property (off-plan or completed) |
| Investment Fund | AED 2M+ in approved investment fund |
| Company Investment | AED 2M+ in UAE company (with min 10% share) |
| Combined Investment | AED 2M+ combination of above |

**Benefits:** 10-year visa, can sponsor family, no minimum salary requirement

### 2. Entrepreneurs (رواد الأعمال)

| Criteria | Requirement |
|----------|-------------|
| Owns Project | Project with min AED 500K capital |
| Project Type | Technical or future sectors preferred |
| Approval | Needs endorsement from UAE economy ministry or incubator |

**Benefits:** 10-year visa, can bring partner and 3 executives

### 3. Specialized Talents (المواهب المتخصصة)

| Category | Requirement |
|----------|-------------|
| Scientists & Researchers | PhD from top 500 universities, notable research |
| Doctors & Specialists | Specialization, publications, GCC license |
| Engineers | Bachelor's+ in engineering, membership in professional body |
| Culture & Art | Award winners, recognized contributions |
| Athletes | Olympic/regional achievements, contracts |
| Media | Award-winning, recognized in field |

**Benefits:** 10-year visa, can sponsor family

### 4. Outstanding Students (الطلاب المتفوقون)

| Category | Requirement |
|----------|-------------|
| University Students | 95%+ in high school, 3.5+ GPA in university |
| Secondary School | 95%+ in Tawjihiya/equivalent |
| International Schools | Top rankings in UAE |

**Benefits:** 5-10 year visa (varies)

---

## Application Process

1. **Prepare Documents** —根据类别准备所需文件
2. **Submit Online** — Via ICP (Federal Authority for Identity and Citizenship) portal
3. **Initial Assessment** — 5-30 days
4. **Approval** — Receive entry permit
5. **Medical & Emirates ID** — Complete in UAE
6. **Visa Stamping** — 10-year residence visa

---

## Required Documents (General)

- Valid passport (min 6 months)
- Passport-sized photo (white background)
- Proof of investment/talent/employment
- Health insurance (UAE valid)
- No criminal record certificate
- For investors: property deed/investment certificate
- For entrepreneurs: business plan, company documents

---

## Key Benefits

| Benefit | Golden Visa Holder |
|---------|-------------------|
| Duration | 10 years (renewable) |
| Sponsorship | Family (spouse, children, parents) |
| Work | No work permit needed |
| Sponsor | No UAE sponsor required |
| Entry | Multiple entry, no limit |
| Status | Not tied to employment |

---

## Processing Time

| Stage | Time |
|-------|------|
| Initial Review | 5-30 days |
| Entry Permit | 2-5 days after approval |
| Medical & ID | 3-5 days |
| Final Visa | 3-5 days |

## Why Invest in Saudi Arabia?

### Key Opportunities

- **Vision 2030** — Massive diversification away from oil
- **NEOM** — $500B mega-city project
- **Giga Projects** — Qiddiya, Red Sea Project, etc.
- **Opening Markets** — 100% foreign ownership now allowed
- **Tax-Free** — No personal income tax

### Key Challenges

- **Cultural adjustment** — Conservative society
- **Language** — Arabic preferred
- **Saudization** — Workforce quotas (Nitaqat)
- **Processing time** — 2-4 weeks for company setup

## Business Setup Overview

### Entity Types Available

| Type | Arabic | Best For |
|------|--------|----------|
| LLC | شركة ذات مسؤولية محدودة | Most foreign investors |
| JSC | شركة مساهمة | Large enterprises |
| Branch | فرع شركة | Existing foreign company |
| Professional Company | شركة مهنية | Consultants |

### Required Licenses

| License | Authority | Purpose |
|---------|-----------|---------|
| MISA License | Ministry of Investment | Foreign investment approval |
| Commercial Registration | Ministry of Commerce | Business license |
| Tax Registration | ZATCA | VAT + Corporate tax |
| Municipality License | Local municipality | Operations permit |

### Estimated Timeline

| Step | Time |
|------|------|
| MISA License | 5-10 days |
| Commercial Registration | 3-7 days |
| Tax Registration | 2-5 days |
| Bank Account | 5-15 days |
| **Total** | **2-4 weeks** |

### Estimated Costs

| Item | Cost (SAR) |
|------|------------|

| Commercial Registration | 2,000-3,000 |
| PRO Services | 1,000-3,000 |

# UAE Free Zones (مناطق حرة)

> **Last Updated:** February 2026
> **Data Sources:** Official Free Zone authorities, Wikipedia, business setup guides

---

## Overview

The UAE has 40+ free zones across Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Sharjah, Ras Al Khaimah, and Fujairah. Each free zone offers:

- 100% foreign ownership
- 0% corporate and personal income tax
- 100% repatriation of capital and profits
- No import/export duties
- Simplified company formation

## Free Zone vs. Mainland

| Factor | Free Zone | Mainland (Onshore) |
|--------|-----------|-------------------|
| Foreign Ownership | 100% | 100% (since 2018) |
| Corporate Tax | 0% (first 5 years extendable) | 9% (post 2023) |
| Customs Duties | 0% | 5% on imports |
| Office Requirement | Yes (virtual available) | Yes |
| Visa Eligibility | Yes (linked to license) | Yes |
| Local Partner Required | No | No (since 2018) |
| Bank Account | Easier | More documentation |
| Location Flexibility | One free zone only | Anywhere in UAE |

## Setup Process (General)

1. **Select Free Zone** — Choose based on activity and budget
2. **Submit Application** — Online via free zone portal
3. **Document Submission** — Passport copies, business plan (some zones)
4. **Pay Fees** — License, office, visa deposits
5. **Receive License** — Usually 5-15 business days
6. **Open Corporate Bank Account** — 2-4 weeks
7. **Apply for Visas** — 2-4 weeks for residence visa

---

## Documents Required

- Completed application form
- Passport copy of shareholder(s)
- Passport-sized photo
- Business description/proposal
- No objection letter (if UAE resident)
- Lease agreement (provided by free zone for flexi-desk)
- Board resolution (for existing companies establishing branch)
